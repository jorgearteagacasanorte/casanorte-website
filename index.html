Frequently asked

Clear answers for property owners.

These direct answers also make the site easier for search engines and AI assistants to understand and cite accurately.

What types of properties does Casa Norte manage?

We manage second homes, vacation homes, long-term rentals, short-term rentals and select commercial properties, including flex and small industrial assets.

How do I receive inspection and maintenance updates?

Management clients receive reports, photos, maintenance updates, service history and documents through our secure client platform, supplemented by direct communication from our team.

Does Casa Norte perform repairs directly?

We coordinate qualified external providers, obtain owner authorization, manage property access and supervise approved work. Provider costs and applicable coordination fees are disclosed separately.

Can services be customized?

Yes. The management scope is based on the property type, inspection frequency, operational needs and the level of involvement the owner wants to delegate.
